About Me

I'm Landen Hawley - a power platform software engineer and musician.

Landen Hawley is a senior at The University Of Wisconsin Oshkosh, and has currently accepted a position as Power Platform Software Engineer at Heartland Business Systems' Little Chute office. He's all about using technology to solve real world personal and business problems and optimize every day workflows.

Other than software, he loves playing the trumpet in jazz and pep band music groups, and has performed at a variety of venues during open mics, jam session, or as a featured instrumentalist in a house band. Another passion for Landen is weight lifting, and cooking, and combining delicious food with perfect macros.

One of Landen's central values that he holds dear is that mentorship is important. He believes that helping others is one of the most fulfilling thing a person can do, which is why he exemplifies this through things like tutoring at the university and reaching out to peers to help when necessary, as well as accepting help himself when he needs it.
